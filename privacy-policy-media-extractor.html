Information Collection and Use

We respect your privacy.

Our core principle is that your data belongs to you. The app functions as a local utility. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information or files on external servers.

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect your name, email address, location, or phone number.

File Access and Permissions

To provide its functionality, the app requires access to the storage on your device.

  • Storage Permission: We request permission to read specific files (documents, presentations, and spreadsheets) that you specifically selected.

  • Saving Data: Extracted media is saved directly on your device’s internal storage. We do not maintain copies of these files once the extraction process is complete.
