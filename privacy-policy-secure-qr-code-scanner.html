Privacy Policy for Secure QR Code Scanner

SmallTinyApps ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our mobile application, Secure QR Code Scanner (the "App").

1. No Data Collection or Transmission

We believe that your data belongs to you. Secure QR Code Scanner is designed to function entirely locally on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information, images, or scanning data.

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifying information.
  • No Usage Tracking: We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking pixels to monitor how you use the App.
  • Local Storage Only: Your scan history is saved directly on your device using a local database. This history never leaves your device and is not synced to the cloud.

2. Device Permissions

To provide its core functionality, the App requires access to specific device permissions.

  • Camera: The App requires access to your device's camera to scan QR codes and barcodes. All image processing and barcode detection (via Google ML Kit) happens locally on your device in real-time. We do not save, record, or upload your camera feed or photos to any external servers.
  • Wi-Fi State: The App requires permission to view and change Wi-Fi states strictly to allow you to automatically connect to Wi-Fi networks when you scan a Wi-Fi QR code.
  • Vibrate: Used to provide haptic feedback when a code is successfully scanned.

3. Security and Threat Analysis

The App includes a local malicious URL scanner to protect you from dangerous links. This feature works entirely offline using a bundled database of known threats. When you scan a link, the security check is performed locally on your device without sending the URL to us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

The App is ad-free and does not integrate any third-party advertising networks or data brokers.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. We are fully compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect contact information, we encourage you to review this policy periodically on our Store Listing.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at smalltinyapps@gmail.com
