Privacy Policy for Pitch the Poop

SmallTinyApps ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our mobile game, Pitch the Poop (the "Game").

1. No Data Collection or Transmission

We believe that your data belongs to you. Pitch the Poop is designed to function entirely locally on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information, gameplay data, or device identifiers.

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifying information.
  • No Usage Tracking: We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking pixels to monitor how you play the Game.
  • Local Storage Only: Your high scores and gameplay history are saved directly on your device using a local database. This history never leaves your device and is not synced to the cloud.

2. Device Permissions

The Game requires absolutely no special device permissions (such as Camera, Microphone, Location, or Storage) to function. It operates entirely within its own secure sandbox on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game is completely offline, ad-free, and does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, data brokers, or analytics services.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our Game does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is designed to be a safe, offline experience suitable for all ages. We are fully compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect contact information, we encourage you to review this policy periodically on our Store Listing.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at smalltinyapps@gmail.com
