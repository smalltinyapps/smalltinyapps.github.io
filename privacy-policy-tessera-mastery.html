Privacy Policy for Tessera Mastery

SmallTinyApps ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Tessera Mastery mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Tessera Mastery is designed as an offline-first application.

Local Data Storage

The App collects and stores gameplay statistics locally on your device to track your progress and performance. This data includes:

  • Game scores and levels achieved
  • Typing speed (Words Per Minute)
  • Accuracy percentages
  • Timestamps of gameplay sessions
  • Information regarding whether the Tessera Keyboard is installed on your device

This data is stored solely on your device using a local database (Android Room). We do not transmit this data to any external servers, and we do not have access to it.

Personal Data

We do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise geographic location.

2. Permissions

The App does not require or request any sensitive permissions, such as access to your contacts, camera, microphone, or location. It does not require Internet access.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, such as:

  • Analytics or tracking SDKs (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics)
  • Advertising networks (e.g., AdMob)
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage or synchronization services

Since the App operates entirely offline, no data is shared with third parties.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on the security of your mobile device itself. We recommend using standard device security features (such as screen locks) to protect your information.

Please note that standard Android Auto Backup services (provided by Google) may include the App's local database in their encrypted cloud backups if you have enabled system-wide backup on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone. Since we do not collect any personal data at all, our App is safe for users of all ages.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at smalltinyapps@gmail.com
