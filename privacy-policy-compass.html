Privacy Policy for Compass

SmallTinyApps ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our mobile application, Pro Compass: Clean & Precise (the "App").

1. No Data Collection

We believe that your data belongs to you. Pro Compass: Clean & Precise is designed to function entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data.

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifying information.
  • No Usage Tracking: We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking pixels to monitor how you use the App.
  • No Location Tracking: While the App uses your device's sensors to determine orientation, it does not track, record, or share your GPS coordinates or physical location history.

2. Device Permissions and Sensors

To provide its core functionality, the App requires access to specific hardware sensors on your device.

  • Magnetometer, Accelerometer, and Gyroscope: Data from these sensors is processed in real-time locally on your device to calculate your heading and orientation. This data is never stored and never leaves your device.
  • Offline Operation: The App does not require an internet connection to function and does not communicate with any external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The App is 100% ad-free and does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, social media plugins, or external data brokers.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. We are fully compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our App. We recommend keeping your device operating system updated to ensure the general security of your hardware.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect contact information, we encourage you to review this policy periodically on our Store Listing.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at smalltinyapps@gmail.com
